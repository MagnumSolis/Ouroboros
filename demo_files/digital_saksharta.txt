PRADHAN MANTRI GRAMIN DIGITAL SAKSHARTA ABHIYAN (PMGDISHA)
GOVERNMENT OF INDIA - MINISTRY OF ELECTRONICS & IT

1. OBJECTIVE
To make 6 crore rural households digitally literate. This scheme aims to bridge the digital divide, specifically targeting the rural population including the marginalized sections of society like Scheduled Castes (SC) / Scheduled Tribes (ST), Minorities, BPL, women and differently-abled persons and minorities.

2. ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA
- Age: The beneficiary should be between 14 to 60 years of age.
- Location: The beneficiary must belong to a rural area.
- One Person Per Household: Only one person from every eligible rural household would be trained.
- Priority: Preference would be given to SC, ST, BPL, women, differently-abled persons and minorities.

3. COURSE DURATION
20 Hours (Minimum 10 Days and Maximum 30 Days).

4. OUTCOMES
After training, the beneficiary should be able to operate digital devices (like Tablets, Smart phones etc.), send and receive emails, browse internet, access Government Services, search for information, undertake digital cashless transactions, etc.
