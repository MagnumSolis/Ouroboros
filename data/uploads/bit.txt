BITS Pilani offers comprehensive finance education through undergraduate (M.Sc. Hons. Economics + Minor in Finance) and postgraduate programs (MBA in FinTech/Finance) across its campuses
. WILP programs provide specialized, industry-focused curricula for working professionals, including financial analytics, risk management, and digital banking technology. 
Key Finance Programs & Offerings

    M.Sc. (Hons.) Economics & Minor in Finance: Available at Goa and Hyderabad campuses, focusing on economic theory and financial application.
    MBA in FinTech (WILP): A Work Integrated Learning Programme designed for working professionals, focusing on the intersection of technology and finance (banking, investment, insurance).
    MBA in Finance (WILP): Focuses on core financial management, requiring relevant experience and a strong background in mathematics/statistics.
    Ph.D. Programme: Offered by the Department of Economics & Finance. 

Key Features

    Curriculum Focus: Strong emphasis on financial analytics, risk management, and technological integration.
    Target Audience: The MBA programs are tailored for working professionals seeking to upgrade skills in finance and technology.
    Eligibility: Requires a 3-year degree or equivalent with relevant work experience (1-2 years).
    Recognition: BITS Pilani is highly ranked for management studies in India.
