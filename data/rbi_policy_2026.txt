RBI Monetary Policy Statement, February 2026

1. Policy Repurchase (Repo) Rate:
The Monetary Policy Committee (MPC) has decided to keep the policy repo rate under the liquidity adjustment facility (LAF) unchanged at 6.50 per cent.

2. Standing Deposit Facility (SDF):
The standing deposit facility (SDF) rate remains unchanged at 6.25 per cent.

3. Marginal Standing Facility (MSF):
The marginal standing facility (MSF) rate and the Bank Rate remain unchanged at 6.75 per cent.

4. Outlook:
The MPC also decided to remain focused on withdrawal of accommodation to ensure that inflation progressively aligns to the target, while supporting growth. These decisions are in consonance with the objective of achieving the medium-term target for consumer price index (CPI) inflation of 4 per cent within a band of +/- 2 per cent, while supporting growth.

5. GDP Growth Projection:
Real GDP growth for 2026-27 is projected at 7.0 per cent with Q1 at 7.2 per cent; Q2 at 6.8 per cent; Q3 at 7.0 per cent; and Q4 at 6.9 per cent.

6. Inflation:
CPI inflation is projected at 4.5 per cent for 2026-27, with Q1 at 5.0 per cent; Q2 at 4.0 per cent; Q3 at 4.6 per cent; and Q4 at 4.7 per cent.
