Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana (PMJDY) - 2026 Update

Objective:
To ensure access to various financial services like availability of basic savings bank account, access to need based credit, remittances facility, insurance and pension to the excluded sections i.e. weaker sections & low income groups.

Benefits:
1. Interest on deposit.
2. Accidental insurance cover of Rs. 2.00 lakh.
3. No minimum balance required.
4. The scheme provide life cover of Rs. 30,000/- payable on death of the beneficiary, subject to fulfillment of the eligibility condition.
5. Easy Transfer of money across India.
6. Beneficiaries of Government Schemes will get Direct Benefit Transfer in these accounts.
7. After satisfactory operation of the account for 6 months, an overdraft facility will be permitted.
8. Access to Pension, insurance products.
9. Personal Accidental Insurance: Under PMJDY, Rupay Debit Card provides personal accidental insurance cover to the accountholders.

Eligibility:
Any individual above the age of 10 years can open a PMJDY account.
Minors can also open the account under the guardianship of natural/legal guardians.

Documents Required:
- Aadhaar Card
- PAN Card
- Voter ID
- Driving License
- NREGA Card
